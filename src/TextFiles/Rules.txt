How to play

Objective: Deplete your entire hand first and win!

1. The dealer deals 13 cards to each player.
2. The player that has the 3 of diamonds goes first.
3. Going in a clockwise direction, each player either: plays a combo or passes.
   (player starting with 3 of diamonds must play the card, with or without a combo).
4. Each subsequent player must play a combo of the same type and must be higher;
   otherwise he/she passes.
5. When all players pass (except the person who played the card(s) ), a new round begins.
   Player must play a card(s).
6. Continue until there is a winner.
